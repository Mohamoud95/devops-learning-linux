# Level 10 to Level 11

## Goal
Decode a password that has been encoded using Base64.

## Concept Explanation
Data is frequently encoded for safe transport across networks (like Base64). Recognizing standard encoding formats and knowing how to decode them is a standard operations skill.

## Commands Used
- `base64`

## What I Learned
I gained an understanding of basic data encoding and how to reverse it directly from the terminal.
