# Level 16 to Level 17

## Goal
Scan a range of ports to find an active service, establish an SSL connection, and submit a password to get an SSH key.

## Concept Explanation
You won't always know which ports are actively listening. Network scanning tools help map out active services before interacting with them securely.

## Commands Used
- `nmap`, `openssl`

## What I Learned
I got hands-on experience with port scanning. Combining service discovery with secure connection negotiation felt like a real-world debugging scenario.
