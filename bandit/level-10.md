# Level 9 to Level 10

## Goal
Extract human-readable strings from a binary file to find a specific password.

## Concept Explanation
Binary files (like executables or raw data) contain mostly unreadable characters. However, they sometimes contain readable text snippets that can be extracted.

## Commands Used
- `strings`, `grep`

## What I Learned
I learned that even compiled or binary files can leak information, and there are tools specifically designed to extract text from them.
